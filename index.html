A progressive, three-phase programme designed to open tight hips, build active mobility, and create lasting change — 10–25 minutes a day.

Start Day 1 →
01–03
Awaken & Release
10–15 min / day

Gently wake the hips up. Build body awareness and identify where tightness lives without forcing anything.

04–07
Open & Strengthen
15–20 min / day

Deeper openers paired with glute activation. Flexible hips need strong glutes — this phase builds both.

08–10
Deepen & Integrate
20–25 min / day

Combine flexibility, strength and stability into full routines. Day 10 benchmarks your progress from Day 1.

Key Principles

⏱
Hold times matter
Stretches under 30 seconds have limited effect. Aim for 45–90 seconds as noted — this is where real change happens.
🔥
Never stretch cold
Do these after a walk, warm-up, or hot shower. Cold muscles resist change and risk injury.
💪
Strength & stretch together
Weak glutes force hip flexors to overwork. Strengthening is just as important as opening.
📅
Consistency beats intensity
15 minutes every day outperforms an hour once a week. Show up — that's the whole plan.
